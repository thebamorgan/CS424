Setup

    1) Remove all exploding kittens from deck and set aside

    2) Remove all of the defuse cards from the deck and deal 1 to each player

    3) Insert the extra defuse cards back into the deck

    4) Shuffle the deck and deal 7 cards face down to each player. Everyone now has a hand of 8 cards (defuse + 7 random cards)

    5) insert n-1 exploding kittens where n is the amount of players

    6) Shuffle deck

    7) Pick a player to go first

Turn

    1) Play a card from hand placing face up on discard pile OR pass

    2) End turn by drawing card from draw pile

Ending Game

    1) Go until only one player hasn't gone boom boom
